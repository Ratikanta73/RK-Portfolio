About Me

Ratikanta Behera is a passionate, creative technologist with a strong foundation in UI/UX design, front-end development, and visual storytelling. His expertise spans modern design tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Framer, allowing him to craft intuitive, user-centered digital experiences.

His innovative mindset is also reflected in his deep exploration of AI tools. He completed a self-driven “30 Days 30 AI Tools” challenge and continues his journey with the Photoshop and Illustrator learning series.

Beyond technical skills, Ratikanta holds leadership positions in various communities, serving as the Campus Creative Officer at Coding Ninjas ITER, Graphic Designer at TFUG Bhubaneswar, and Design Lead at GDG On Campus ITER. He actively leads events, design sprints, and content initiatives that blend creativity with technology.
